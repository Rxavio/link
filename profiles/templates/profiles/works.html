Account

1.Register your account

Enter your username, your valid email, password and confirm password.

2.Login with your account Credentials

To login enter your username and password

3.Update your profile

On dropdown link under your profile icon click my profile fill all required informations and upload the images then save changes
we are highly recommend you to update your profile with your valid email,phone number and update your profile image with your own image because this will be so useful on this patform.

4.Change your Password

On dropdown link under your profile icon click password Enter your old password and then enter new password and confirm new password then confirm changes.

5.Reset Password(Forget)

Under Login button click on Forget password link enter your valid email, click the link on your email, enter new password and confirm new password.

6.Logout

This is your own account security, logout everytime especially when you are using public computers (Cyber Cafe,colleges,schools...).

Profiles

1.Available

you did not send invite to anybody, you did not receive an invite of anybody, this mean you are available to everyone on the platform,you can navigate to all Available profile, see the details of any profile and send him/her an invite vs you can receive an invite from other profile anytime.

2.Waiting

you've sent an invite to any profile but not response yet this profile will be on your waiting profile list and you are not available to other. you have to wait this profile respond you or cancel invite.

3.My invite

you've received an invite of other profile, which is on the list on your invite you can see the datails of the profile,you can accept this invite and also you can reject this invite.

4.Coupled

After some hours,minutes or seconds the invite you sent to any profile got accepted or after accepting the invite of other profile, this profile is on coupled list,you can view the details,you can end the relationship with this profile and the main important you can do booking.

Booking

you are definitely coupled, and you want to meet with this person you coupled with, click booking your space button, Navigate to the list of table where you can choose one table and book the free hour of your choice.
Note: Email and Telephone Number are so required to be in touch with you, we are highly recommend you to update your profile informations with your valid Email and Telephone number. To arrange the meeting we will always call the coupled profiles and ask them what they wish.
